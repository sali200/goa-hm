About Kristina

Kristina Carrillo-Bucaram, known as FullyRaw Kristina, is a health advocate and the founder of the FullyRaw movement. She has inspired millions around the world to embrace a fully raw, plant-based lifestyle through her motivational speaking, books, and social media presence. Her journey started with a personal transformation, and now she helps others achieve their health goals through fully raw diets and holistic approaches to wellness.
